ABOUT US

We provide Finance and Accounting Outsourcing Services that meet our client's Order-to Cash (O2C), Procure-to-Pay (P2P), and Record-to-Report (R2R) requirements. We have in-house tax and legal experts that provide Tax Compliance and Advisory Services to assess and provide tax avoidance strategies as well as BIR representation for LOA cases.
We started the company by helping Filipino entrepreneurs register their businesses, obtain mandatory requirements, assist with bookkeeping and tax compliance, as well as provide financial advice. We give our clients access to affordable and cost-effective back-office and consultancy services necessary to support and grow their businesses.
Eventually, as word of our customer-focused services spread, we gained traction by assisting newly organized, as well as established, construction companies with their PCAB licenses and other documentary requirements. We also work closely with their existing, and oftentimes non-existent, accounting department to avoid and remedy tax deficiencies as well as comply with the necessary regulatory requirements.
Our business rapidly grew because we consistently surpass client expectations. Our clients recognize the value of our services brought to these engagements. This is our commitment to “empower entrepreneurs and seek to maintain their loyalty and trust through sound tax and compliance risk mitigation services and advice”.
We will continue to find innovative solutions and enable our clients to drive their passion, focus on their determination to succeed, realize their dreams, and attain financial freedom for sustainable nation-building.
